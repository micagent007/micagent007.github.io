I am currently a PhD student at Ecole Nationale des Ponts et Chaussées [CERMICS](https://cermics-lab.enpc.fr/) under the supervision of [Frédéric Meunier](https://fred-meunier.github.io), [Claudia d'Ambrosio](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=GyQH2p4AAAAJ&hl=en) and [Jordi Badosa](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=8BsdsqMAAAAJ&hl=ca). I'm working on the [Ifker project for climate](https://www.polytechnique.edu/sites/default/files/content/communiques/fichiers/2025-05/CP-SmartGTE.pdf), an initiative of the interdisciplinary center Energy4Climate ([E4C](https://e4c.ip-paris.fr)). More precisely, my thesis is about the management of a geothermal pump coupled with solar panels in order to optimize daily consumption and seasonal efficiency.

I'm a freshly graduated student from Ecole Des Ponts ([ENPC](https://ecoledesponts.fr)) with a master's degree in Operations Research ([MPRO](https://uma.ip-paris.fr/mpro)).

Research Topics

* Combinatorial optimization
* Stochastic optimization
* Decision-dependent uncertainties
* Machine learning
* Reinforcement learning
